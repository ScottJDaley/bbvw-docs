# ![icon](../img/items/water-stone.png) Water stone

**Category:** Evolution

## Description
Evolves an Eevee into Vaporeon, a Lombre into Ludicolo, a Panpour into Simipour, a Poliwhirl into Poliwrath, a Shellder into Cloyster, or a Staryu into Starmie.
