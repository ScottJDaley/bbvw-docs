# Grass-pledge

**Type:** Grass  
**Category:** Special  
**Power:** 80  
**Accuracy:** 100  
**PP:** 10  

## Description
With Fire Pledge, damages opposing Pokémon for 1/8 their max HP every turn for four turns.
