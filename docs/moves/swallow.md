# Swallow

**Type:** ![normal](../img/types/normal.png)  
**Category:** ![status](../img/types/status.png)  
**Power:** None  
**Accuracy:** None  
**PP:** 10  

## Description
Recovers 1/4 HP after one Stockpile, 1/2 HP after two Stockpiles, or full HP after three Stockpiles.
