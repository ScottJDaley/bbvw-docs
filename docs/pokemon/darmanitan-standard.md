## Evolution

## Abilities
- **[Sheer force](abilities/sheer-force.md)**: Strengthens moves with extra effects to 1.3× their power, but prevents their extra effects.
- **[Zen mode](abilities/zen-mode.md)**: Changes darmanitan's form after each turn depending on its HP: Zen Mode below 50% max HP, and Standard Mode otherwise.
