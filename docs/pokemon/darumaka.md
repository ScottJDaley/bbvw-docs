## Evolution

## Abilities
- **[Hustle](abilities/hustle.md)**: Strengthens physical moves to inflict 1.5× damage, but decreases their accuracy to 0.8×.
- **[Rattled](abilities/rattled.md)**: Raises Speed one stage upon being hit by a dark, ghost, or bug move.
