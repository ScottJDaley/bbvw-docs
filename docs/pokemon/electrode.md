## Abilities
- **[Aftermath](abilities/aftermath.md)**: Damages the attacker for 1/4 its max HP when knocked out by a contact move.
- **[Static](abilities/static.md)**: Has a 30% chance of paralyzing attacking Pokémon on contact.
