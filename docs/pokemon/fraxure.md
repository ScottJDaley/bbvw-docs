## Evolution
No changes from base games.

## Abilities
- **[Rivalry](abilities/rivalry.md)**: Increases damage inflicted to 1.25× against Pokémon of the same gender, but decreases damage to 0.75× against the opposite gender.
- **[Mold breaker](abilities/mold-breaker.md)**: Bypasses targets' abilities if they could hinder or prevent a move.
- **[Unnerve](abilities/unnerve.md)**: Prevents opposing Pokémon from eating held Berries.
