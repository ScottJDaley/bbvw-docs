## Evolution
No changes from base games.

## Abilities
- **[Early bird](abilities/early-bird.md)**: Makes sleep pass twice as quickly.
- **[Flash fire](abilities/flash-fire.md)**: Protects against fire moves.  Once one has been blocked, the Pokémon's own Fire moves inflict 1.5× damage until it leaves battle.
- **[Unnerve](abilities/unnerve.md)**: Prevents opposing Pokémon from eating held Berries.
