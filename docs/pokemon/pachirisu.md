## Evolution

## Abilities
- **[Volt Absorb](abilities/volt-absorb.md)**: Absorbs electric moves, healing for 1/4 max HP.
- **[Pickup](abilities/pickup.md)**: Picks up other Pokémon's used and Flung held items.  May also pick up an item after battle.
