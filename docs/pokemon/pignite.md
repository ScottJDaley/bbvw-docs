## Abilities
- **[Blaze](abilities/blaze.md)**: Strengthens fire moves to inflict 1.5× damage at 1/3 max HP or less.
- **[Adaptability](abilities/adaptability.md)**: Increases the same-type attack bonus from 1.5× to 2×.
