## Evolution
**Base Game:** First Stage  
**New:** Slowbro: Level 37 or Level Up with a Shellder in the party.
**New:** Slowking: Level Up at Day with King's Rock.

## Abilities
- **[Regenerator](abilities/regenerator.md)**: Heals for 1/3 max HP upon switching out.
- <span class="change-new-label">NEW</span> **[Drizzle](abilities/drizzle.md)**: Summons rain that lasts indefinitely upon entering battle.
