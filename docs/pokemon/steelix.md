## Evolution
No changes from base games.

## Abilities
- **[Sheer Force](abilities/sheer-force.md)**: Strengthens moves with extra effects to 1.3× their power, but prevents their extra effects.
- **[Sturdy](abilities/sturdy.md)**: Prevents being KOed from full HP, leaving 1 HP instead.  Protects against the one-hit KO moves regardless of HP.
