## Evolution

## Abilities
- **[Poison point](abilities/poison-point.md)**: Has a 30% chance of poisoning attacking Pokémon on contact.
- **[Swarm](abilities/swarm.md)**: Strengthens bug moves to inflict 1.5× damage at 1/3 max HP or less.
- **[Speed boost](abilities/speed-boost.md)**: Raises Speed one stage after each turn.
