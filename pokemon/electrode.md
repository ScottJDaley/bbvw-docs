## Type
<span class="type-badge type-electric">electric</span>

## Abilities
- **Aftermath**: Damages the attacker for 1/4 its max HP when knocked out by a contact move.
- **Static**: Has a 30% chance of paralyzing attacking Pokémon on contact.
